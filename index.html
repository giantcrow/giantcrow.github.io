Intro

Our goal really is simple, make games that we like to play and in turn hoping that we'll end up having something you'll enjoy as well. If you want to try out our games you can check it out here.

That said, our dream is still to create games that will be part of the gaming history alongside games as Crash Bandicoot, Final Fanatasy, Monster Hunter, Undertale, Sonic, Mario, MGS, Resident Evil etc. I hope we can make better and better games but we need your support as well to make our games come true. As much as this is for us fellow gamer's feedback is also one of the things we take to our hearts.

Released Games

Pixeling

The first game created by us, using the basis of a whack-a-mole game, we sought out to create a game that has more elements compared to the original. The game features 4 major stages each with an individual boss battle and multiple unique monsters to fight against.

Starry Light

A small fun project trying to create an infinite runner with some unique mechanics and a play on words of Starry Night.

Founder

Freeman Kam

Freeman is the founder of the Giant Crow Studio, and is currently working on these games independently. Since he was a kid, he has been creating games of all sorts, from inventing/modifying playground games, getting featured on newspapers for homemade board games, creating gigantic WC3 maps to professionally being a game developer, Freeman has always been very passionate about making video games.

As a graduate from Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo in 2015 has allowed Freeman to pursue game development as a professional.

During his years at the University of Waterloo as a Computer Engineer, he was exposed to many work places and have learnt vast amounts of knowledge from different businesses, and even getting to work Lumosity Labs' game development team.

After his graduation in 2015, he started working for Game Hive Corp, making games such as Beat the Boss 4, Tap Tycoon, Tap Titans 2.
